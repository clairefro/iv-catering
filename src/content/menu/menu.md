### Rental Items & Service

All rental items for your occasion will be rented through Special Occasions in Corvallis. Payment goes directly to Special Occasions. Iovino's provides all service wares for food buffets.

### Bar Service

Iovino’s does not supply bar service. We work with other catering companies to provide this service for you. We can provide all other non-alcoholic beverages.

### Costs

Iovino's charges a 20% Labor cost on total Food Cost

A 50% Deposit along with your confirmed guest count is due two weeks prior to event. We accept checks, cash, no credit cards.

Staff working your event - $20.00 per hour (includes traveling time)

No mileage charge for events within a 25 mile radius (.85 cents per mile if exceeds 25 miles)
